# Chapter 1, the start.
Early November (shitvival phase, 2014E era):

So like most revivals (shitvivals) I didnt really care about the project lol at all, I wasn't even confident id get it working whatsoever. Especially considering I had no experience in modding clients as much as you need to for revivals. Most of this phase was just research, unfortunately I made the naive mistake of using source files from a project called Roblox Filtering Disabled, at the time I

1) I had no idea of the controversy
2) I really didn't really care about the project since it was so early that I just wanted to test and meme around

# Chapter 1.5, the start 2 electric boogaloo.

Early December (shitvivalish phase, 2016L era):

I was still skeptical of the project but I saw that it had great potential, after the whole reddit incident it did discourage me a bit but I knew I had to keep going to keep it alive. This pushed me to start taking the project 100% seriously, I started working virtually day and night (even to this day i've updated the site everyday one way or another) to provide a decent experience.
